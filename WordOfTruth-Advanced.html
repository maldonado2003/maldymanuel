Committed to build the future,
for more than 40 years.

Since 1973 Tirrena Scavi operates in many different countries around the globe, with a growing commitment and focus. Tirrena Scavi is a building company specialized in Civil Engineering Works, Waterworks and Building & Industrial Works.

1Experience

Committed
to build the future, all over the world.

From the beginning, Tirrena Scavi placed great attention on foreign markets. In 1975, started its first work experience abroad with a subsidiary branch in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, where, for the next 10 years, it carried out major road infrastructure projects. Other important civil engineering projects were undertaken in the Republic of Slovenia, in Egypt and in Romania, where an efficient business organization allowed Tirrena Scavi to execute complex and varied projects all over the country.
